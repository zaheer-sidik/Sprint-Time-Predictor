Training Time Predictor - Sprints

Calculate your predicted sprint times for different distances based on your Personal Best. Select a distance you've run, enter your time, and see your estimated times for other sprint distances. The data this calculator is based on comes from Sprints and Relays by Dick, Frank W (1987) These times are predictions and may vary based on individual performance.
Note: The 60m, 100m, 200m and 400m times are based on competition data (electronic timing), whereas the other distances use hand timing.
